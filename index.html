Risemont Education Limited is a Hong Kong company that designs and publishes educational software for young children and their families. We build learning experiences that are warm, ad-free, and age-appropriate — helping children prepare for primary school with confidence.

Our product

Our flagship app, DreamUp Adventure (升夢大冒險), is a native iOS learning companion for children aged 4–6. It brings together Chinese (Cantonese and Mandarin), English, and mathematics in themed adventures designed for Hong Kong families preparing for primary-school interviews and early primary education.

  • Available on iPhone and iPad
  • No advertisements and no tracking of children
  • Designed for pre-readers, with clear guidance for parents

關於我們

Risemont Education Limited 是一間總部位於香港的教育科技公司，專為幼童及其家庭設計及發佈教育軟件。我們致力打造溫暖、無廣告、適齡的學習體驗，協助孩子自信地為升小做好準備。

我們的產品

我們的旗艦應用程式 DreamUp Adventure（升夢大冒險） 是一款專為 4 至 6 歲兒童而設的 iOS 學習夥伴。應用程式將中文（廣東話及普通話）、英文與數學，融入主題式冒險之中，切合香港家庭升小面試及幼小銜接的需要。

  • 適用於 iPhone 及 iPad
  • 無廣告，亦不會追蹤兒童
  • 專為未識字幼童而設，並為家長提供清晰指引
